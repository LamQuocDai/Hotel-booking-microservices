## Double-Check Pattern Explanation

```
????????????????????????????????????????????????????????
?  Why Recheck Inside Transaction?                     ?
?                                                       ?
?  Timeline without recheck:                           ?
?  ???????????????????????????????????????????????     ?
?  T1: Request A checks availability ? AVAILABLE       ?

?  T5: Request A releases lock                         ?
?  T6: Request B acquires lock                         ?
?  T7: Request B creates booking ? DOUBLE BOOKING!    ?
?                                                       ?
?  Timeline WITH recheck:                              ?
?  ???????????????????????????????????????????????     ?
?  T1: Request A checks availability ? AVAILABLE       ?
?  T2: Request B checks availability ? AVAILABLE       ?
?  T3: Request A acquires lock                         ?
?  T4: Request A RECHECKS ? AVAILABLE                  ?
?  T5: Request A creates booking                       ?
?  T6: Request A releases lock                         ?
?  T7: Request B acquires lock                         ?
?  T8: Request B RECHECKS ? NOT AVAILABLE ?            ?
?  T9: Request B returns 409 Conflict                  ?
```
